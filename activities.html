Activity

▼ European Projects

The organization focused on developing and implementing a wide range of projects on a European level.

Each of our European projects has specific objectives and comes to fulfill the needs that youth signals, with support from the network partnerships from countries all over Europe.

See the projects

▼ Personal Development Courses

The NGO tries constantly to research and to great the expectations of the youth.

We organize regularly courses within the areas that are vital nowadays in the community: leadership, conflict management, entrepreneurship, healthy lifestyle, sports, etc.

▼ Summer Camps

We made a tradition on organizing summer camps, because we believe in the natural desire of children to explore the world and we want to be part of it.

Our volunteers will provide the access to activities which stimulate the powerful resources children already have, in a healthy environment.

▼ Cultural Activities

There are a few initiatives in Romania for artistic and cultural education in general. But we believe that young artists and the future people within the cultural area need support and recognition from the society.

That is way, through our cultural events in art, history, literature, we want to stimulate the performance and to encourage youth of great value.

▼ Team Building

Nothing in the world can go well without great communities.

. Communication, relationship, acquiring skills, getting out of the comfort zone, involvement in social life, meeting new people, understanding of diversity, team work- these are the matters we bring out for the youth, in our team buildings activities, in order to create better communities.

Our project team has expertise in projects for young people from disadvantaged backgrounds and fewer opportunities such as:

- educational difficulties (ED): young people with learning difficutlies: early school-leavers; low qualified adults

- economic obstacles (EO): people with a low standard of living, low income, dependence on social welfare system or homeless; young people in long-term unemployment or poverty

- cultural differences (CD): people belonging to a national or ethnic minority (roma);
